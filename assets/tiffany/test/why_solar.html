Utilizing solar energy offers a limitless supply of clean, safe, renewable energy with little to no maintenance. By harnessing the sun’s rays into usable power, we broaden the gap with our dependence on fossil fuels. And if doing your part for Mother Earth isn’t enough, recent market swings and availability of materials have made solar photovoltaic systems more affordable than ever. From a mere fiscal standpoint, investing in solar technology makes sense, and cents, too!

Government incentives and tax rebates and credits are paving the way for savvy homeowners and businesses to distance themselves from their looming energy bills. With the rate caps slowly vanishing from local providers, now is the time to take the green step forward.
